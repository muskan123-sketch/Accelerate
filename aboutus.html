During this pandemic the educational sector faced lots of problems which went unnoticed. There was a huge shift in the teaching as well as in the learning process. We conducted surveys of several teachers who faced this problem of adapting the virtual platforms for teaching. After the survey we analysed their problems, went through some stages of design thinking, came up with some conclusions and tried to resolve some problems by making updates in the existing platforms. We also kept in mind the situations faced by students while adapting this virtual method of learning.
